Entity Framing Taxonomy

Key Contributions

  • Release of a novel multilingual dataset with hierarchical entity‐role annotations.
  • A comprehensive taxonomy of 22 archetypes under three main roles.
  • Detailed corpus statistics across languages, domains, and roles.
  • Benchmarks using fine‐tuned multilingual transformers and hierarchical zero‐shot LLMs.

Dataset

  • Languages: Bulgarian, English, Hindi, European Portuguese, Russian
  • Domains: Ukraine-Russia War, Climate Change
  • Annotations: 5,800+ entity mentions labeled with roles
  • Novel Taxonomy: 22 fine-grained roles under three main categories
